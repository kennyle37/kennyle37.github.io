September 16, 2023

Kenny Le and Victoria Tong's Wedding

Join Us!

Introduction

Esteemed friends and beloved family, we cordially invite you to be a part of the most significant and joyous occasion of our lives as Kenny Le and Victoria Tong exchange vows at the grand and opulent Fairmont Hotel in San Francisco.

On this unforgettable day, we extend our warmest invitation to our family and friends for you to witness the ceremony at 4:00pm in the exquisite Pavilion & Loggia hall.

As the sun sets, we welcome you to raise a glass and indulge in a cocktail hour on the breathtaking Roof Garden.

As the night unfolds, we invite you to dine with us in the grand and majestic Crown Room, where we will watch the sunset together as Mr. & Mrs.

This is a day that we have been dreaming of and planning for, and we cannot wait to share it with you. Please be punctual as we want to start the ceremony on time. We look forward to celebrating this milestone with you.



Entrée Options

We are excited to share a culinary journey with you starting with our cocktail hour delights of Dungeness Crab Sliders with Cornichons and Louie Dressing.

For dinner, we will be starting off with Dungeness Crab & Cauliflower soup with snipped chives.

For our main entree, we are offering the choices of fish, poultry, or vegan options:

1) Fish: Grilled California Sea Bass with Baby Bok Choy, Thai Yellow Curry, Coconut Milk, Mint, Cilantro, Ginger, and Saffron Jasmine Rice

2) Poultry: Oven Roasted Chicken marinated with duck fat roasted Potatoes, Roasted Butternut Squash, Adobo, and Chicken Jus

3) Vegan Rataouille Ravioli with Tomato Sugo, Ricotta (the pasta is vegan; a vegan “cheese” is available)

Last but not least, we will end this experience with our delicious Wedding Cake.

Directions

For the best travel experience, it is recommended that guests carpool or use a ride-sharing service such as Uber or Lyft to the venue. Parking options are available in nearby garages, and street parking is strongly discouraged.





Parking

Fairmont San Francisco Hotel Garage is located on the corner of Powell and California Streets. Parking for visitors is $19 per hour up to 4+ hours at the rate of $68 tax included.
