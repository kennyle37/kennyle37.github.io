About Me!

TLDR: Fullstack Software Engineer, likes learning, eating, and traveling

Hi, my name is Kenny!

I am a full stack software engineer with a passion for developing clean, modular, and scalable applications. I have a growth driven mentality and love to tackle difficult challenges that can fuel my passion as a developer. I strive for improvements daily, whether it's solving problems on Leetcode and HackerRank, or a personal project, I take every opportunity as a learning experience.

On the front end, I have experience with React and AngularJS, as well as frameworks that accompany these libraries such as NextJS. On the back end, I have experience with using Postgres, MySQL, and Mongo databases. I also have experience with using NodeJS, Express, Apollo, ObjectionJS, and GraphQL to create and access RESTful APIs and service-oriented architecture. My tool belt also includes CSS and SASS, HTML, Semantic-UI, JEST, test-driven-development, agile methodology, and can pick up additional frameworks and/or libraries as needed for a successful development process.

My free time is split between playing video games, watching movies, getting boba, reading programming books, or some fantasy or science fiction novel. I am a travel enthusiast and love to experience different cultures.
